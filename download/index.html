ZCity

Garry's Mod - US East - Virginia

Z-City is a Garry's Mod server featuring Homicide, TDM, and more. Realistic physics, bleeding systems, ragdoll control, and a well-thought-out weapon system. Built different from Homigrad.

Getting Started

All addons are delivered automatically via Steam Workshop. When you join for the first time, your client will download everything it needs. No manual setup required.

First-join download time depends on your connection speed. Expect 5-15 minutes on a standard connection. Subsequent joins will be near-instant.

What Sets Z-City Apart

Compared to its predecessor Homigrad, Z-City differs both visually and mechanically. Gameplay is significantly slower and more tactical. Realistic body physics, a dynamic bleeding system, and ragdoll control change how every encounter plays out.

This is not a spray-and-pray server. Read the rules, play smart, and respect the other players.
